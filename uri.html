Uri: The Surgical Strike(2019)

Storyline: Uri: The Surgical Strike follows the gripping and intense journey of Major Vihaan Singh Shergill (Vicky Kaushal), who is part of the Indian Army’s elite Special Forces. The film begins with a harrowing ambush in Chandel, Manipur, in which Major Vihaan’s unit is attacked by militants from the Nationalist Socialist Council of Nagaland (NSCN). Despite the tragedy, Vihaan leads a successful counter-attack that avenges the loss of his comrades. As the story progresses, Vihaan takes a desk job in New Delhi to care for his mother, Suhashini (Swaroop Sampat), who is terminally ill. However, fate brings him back into the action when he becomes involved with Jasmine D'Almeida (Yami Gautam), a covert intelligence officer working to uncover a conspiracy linked to the terrorist attacks in the Kashmir Valley. The story reaches a turning point when, in 2016, the Indian Army suffers a devastating terrorist attack on their base in Uri, Jammu & Kashmir, resulting in the loss of 19 soldiers. The nation is shaken by the violence, and the government decides to respond with a covert operation. Vihaan, now fully re-engaged with his military roots, leads a group of commandos in a highly secretive and dangerous surgical strike across the Line of Control (LoC). The mission’s objective is to eliminate the terrorists responsible for the Uri attack, and it requires exceptional precision and planning. Using advanced satellite surveillance, drones, and intelligence inputs, the strike is executed with extreme precision. Vihaan and his team embark on this perilous journey, battling extreme conditions and enemy forces, all while upholding their duty to the nation. The strike is successful, but the emotional weight of the mission and the loss of lives continue to haunt Vihaan, even as he and his team return victorious. The film ends on a note of national pride and triumph, with Major Vihaan paying tribute to the sacrifices made by his fallen comrades. Uri: The Surgical Strike is a poignant story of bravery, sacrifice, and patriotism, and it beautifully portrays the valor and determination of India's armed forces.
