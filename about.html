About Upstream

Upstream is a free streaming platform designed to make high-quality films and videos accessible to everyone — no subscriptions, no downloads, no hassles.

We curate and embed publicly available movies from sources like YouTube, organizing them into categories and collections to help you discover great content quickly and easily.

Our mission is to empower a community of cinephiles who can watch, share, and even contribute to the platform. With Upstream, you're not just a viewer — you're part of a movement to bring free, meaningful entertainment to the world.

Whether you're diving into indie masterpieces, hunting for cult classics, or exploring hidden gems, Upstream is here to guide your journey through the world of free cinema.

Stream. Share. Support Cinema. That’s Upstream.
