Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is UpStream?

UpStream is a free online streaming platform that allows users to watch movies and TV shows embedded from YouTube—all in one place.

How do I sign up for UpStream?

Currently, UpStream does not require a signup. Just visit the site and start streaming.

Is UpStream free to use?

Yes, UpStream is completely free. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.

Do I need an account to watch content?

No account is required. All content is accessible without logging in.

What devices can I use to stream on UpStream?

You can stream on any device with a browser—phones, tablets, laptops, and smart TVs.
