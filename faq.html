UpStream FAQs

Account & Settings

How do I reset my password?

Currently, UpStream doesn’t require user accounts, so there are no passwords to reset.

Can I change my email address?

There’s no email registration required at this time.

How do I delete my account?

UpStream does not store personal user accounts, so no deletion is necessary.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes. We do not collect or store personal information on UpStream.

Can I manage multiple profiles?

Since accounts are not required, profile management is not available.

Content And Streaming

Can I watch Upstream on my TV?

Yes, if your TV has a browser or supports screen casting from a device, you can access Upstream.

Does Upstream host any original content?

No, Upstream embeds content available publicly on YouTube and does not host original media.

How can I contact Upstream?

You can reach us via email at contact@upstream.com.

Is there a subscription plan?

No, Upstream is completely free and does not offer any subscription plans.

Are there ads on Upstream?

We do not run our own ads, but embedded YouTube content may contain YouTube ads.

Can I contribute to Upstream as a developer?

Yes, we welcome contributions. Contact us to learn about opportunities to collaborate.

Can I embed Upstream movies on my own site?

You can embed the original YouTube videos, but not the Upstream interface itself.

Does Upstream collect any personal data?

No, we do not collect or store personal data from users.

How can I give feedback?

You can send your feedback to feedback@upstream.com.

Privacy And Safety

Is registration available for future features?

We are considering optional user accounts in future updates for personalized features.

Do I need a VPN to use Upstream?

No VPN is needed unless YouTube itself is restricted in your region.

Is Upstream available in all countries?

Yes, as long as YouTube is accessible in your country, Upstream will work.

How do I suggest a new feature?

Email us your ideas at ideas@upstream.com.

What browsers support Upstream?

All modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari are supported.

Does Upstream have a dark mode?

Yes, Upstream supports dark mode automatically based on your system settings.

Can I share movies with friends?

Yes, you can share the YouTube video links directly or share our page link.

Is there a limit to how many movies I can watch?

No, you can watch unlimited movies as long as they are available on YouTube.

What kind of movies are on Upstream?

We curate a mix of classics, documentaries, indie films, and public domain movies.

Who curates the content on Upstream?

Our small team selects movies based on quality and availability on YouTube.

Quries And Suggestion

Can I request removal of a video?

If you're a content owner, contact us and we will remove any embedded video upon request.

Does Upstream have closed captions?

Closed captions depend on whether the original YouTube video has them enabled.

Can I use Upstream for educational purposes?

Yes, our curated content can be a great resource for learning and discussion.

Is there a newsletter for updates?

We’re planning a newsletter soon! Stay tuned by checking our homepage.

Can I share my movie list with others?

Currently, we don’t support user lists, but this may be added in future versions.

How are the movies categorized?

Movies are categorized by genre, theme, and popularity.

How do I report an issue?

Send an email to support@upstream.com with the issue details.

What makes Upstream different?

We focus on curated, free content embedded from YouTube, no fluff or subscriptions.

Do you offer curated playlists?

Yes, we have themed playlists available for browsing on the homepage.

Is there a way to request subtitles?

We cannot add subtitles directly, but you can use YouTube’s built-in subtitle tools.

Features And Navigation

Does Upstream work on mobile devices?

Yes, the site is mobile-friendly and works on all modern smartphones and tablets.

Do you have a favorites or watchlist feature?

Not yet, but we are exploring this for future releases.

How can I become a community moderator?

We’ll post openings on our site when community moderation becomes available.

Do you allow user reviews?

Not at the moment, but a review and rating system is in development.

Can I download the movies?

No, downloading is not supported. All content is streamed directly from YouTube.

Are parental controls available?

We currently rely on YouTube’s own content filtering. Direct parental controls are in planning.

Can I search for specific genres on Upstream?

Yes, our homepage allows you to filter movies by genre for easier browsing.

Are short films included on Upstream?

Yes, we feature a variety of short films alongside full-length movies.

Does Upstream support 4K streaming?

Streaming quality depends on the original YouTube upload. If a video is available in 4K, you can watch it in 4K.

Can I donate to support Upstream?

We’re working on a donation option. In the meantime, sharing the platform helps a lot!

Other Questions

How are movies selected for the homepage?

Our team selects movies based on genre diversity, quality, and community feedback.

Is there a way to filter out horror or explicit content?

Currently, we do not have filtering tools, but we're looking to add content preferences soon.

Can I use Upstream for film club meetings?

Yes, Upstream is great for group watching and film discussions.

Do you feature seasonal content?

Yes, we feature themed playlists during holidays and special events.
