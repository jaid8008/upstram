Mission Raniganj: The Great Bharat Rescue(2023)

Storyline: Mission Raniganj is a 2023 Hindi-language disaster thriller based on the real-life events of the 1989 Raniganj coal mine disaster in West Bengal. The film chronicles the heroic efforts of mining engineer Jaswant Singh Gill (Akshay Kumar), who led a daring rescue mission to save 65 miners trapped in a flooded coal mine.​ IMDb +5 Rotten Tomatoes +5 Wikipedia +5 The narrative unfolds in the small village of Raniganj, where the community is closely knit, and the men work in the nearby coalfields. One fateful night, while 250 workers are in the mines, a sudden flood traps 70 of them underground. Jaswant Singh Gill, upon learning of the disaster, rushes to the site, leaving his pregnant wife Nirdosh (Parineeti Chopra) at home.​ IMDb At the mine, Jaswant assesses the situation and devises a rescue plan. However, he faces opposition from corrupt officials, including Senior Engineer Ujwal, who are more concerned with personal vendettas than the lives of the trapped miners. Despite these challenges, Jaswant remains determined and works tirelessly to execute the rescue operation.​Netflix +6 IMDb +6 Rotten Tomatoes+6 Deep within the mine, senior workers like Pasu and Shaligram encourage their fellow miners to stay hopeful and reach the top of the mine, believing in the possibility of rescue.
