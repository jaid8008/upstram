Aparichit (2006)

Storyline: Aparichit is a psychological action thriller that explores the life of Ramanujam "Ambi" Iyengar, a perfectionist lawyer who battles the rampant corruption in society. His frustration with injustice triggers a rare psychological disorder, leading him to develop two alter egos: Remo, a flamboyant model, and Aparichit, a relentless vigilante. Aparichit uses brutal methods based on ancient scriptures to punish wrongdoers, while Ambi remains unaware of his split identities. As a series of mysterious punishments rock the city, law enforcement closes in, and Ambi's hidden mental struggle surfaces. The story captures the emotional and moral complexities of seeking justice outside the legal system, culminating in a powerful journey of psychological healing, societal reflection, and a subtle warning that Aparichit's spirit may never truly fade.
