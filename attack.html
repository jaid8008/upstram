Attack: Part 1 (2022)

Storyline: Attack: Part 1 is a 2022 Indian Hindi-language science fiction action thriller that introduces a groundbreaking narrative in Indian cinema. The film follows Major Arjun Shergill, an Indian Army officer who becomes India's first cybernetic super-soldier. After a terrorist attack leaves him paralyzed and claims the life of his beloved Ayesha, Arjun volunteers for a government project that integrates advanced artificial intelligence into human soldiers. Equipped with an AI assistant named IRA, Arjun embarks on a mission to thwart a major terrorist threat. The film delves into themes of patriotism, sacrifice, and the ethical implications of merging technology with human life, all set against high-octane action sequences and a gripping storyline.​
